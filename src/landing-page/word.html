1. verb. desert (a cause, a country or an army), often in order to join the opposing cause, country, or army ffffffffffffff.

2. verb. leave someone who needs or counts on you; leave in the lurch.

3. noun. arid land with little or no vegetation.

4. verb. leave behind.

5. noun. (usually plural) a person's deservingness of or entitlement to reward or punishment.

Examples

1. "If soldiers deserted Hitler's army, they were shot".

2. "The mother deserted her children".

3. "the students deserted the campus after the end of exam period".
